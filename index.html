A Love Worth Telling

Our Story

How It Started

If you asked us back then if this would become forever, we would have smiled and stayed silent. We did not see it coming. We were not even looking.

We first met in November 2021 at a prayer meeting. A brief exchange. Nothing loud. Nothing memorable on the surface. But something settled quietly that neither of us could explain.

Months later, in April 2022, we met again in Abuja at a youth conference. Same city, different version of us. What started as simple conversations became something we looked forward to without realizing why. Sitting together, talking endlessly, laughing too easily, praying without forcing it… it all felt strangely familiar, like something already written.

We did not fall in love at once. It grew slowly, almost silently, until it became impossible to ignore.

We became friends. Then best friends. Then the person we could not go a day without.

And somewhere in between, without a single announcement, we became home to each other.

✦

How It's Going

Now we look back and realize nothing about this was ordinary.

What started as chance meetings has become a love that feels like peace. Like safety. Like coming back to yourself in another person.

We pray together. We grow together. We laugh until it hurts. We sit in silence and still feel understood. Even distance never really separated us, because somehow we always found our way back into each other's thoughts, voices, and hearts.

We did not force this. We did not rush it. It became what it was meant to be.

A love we recognize as grace. A bond we cannot unsee. A future we no longer imagine separately.

And now, surrounded by love, prayers, and everyone who has walked with us, we are stepping into forever with full hearts and steady hands.

We found home in each other. And we are not letting go.

The revelation is still unfolding ❤️‍🔥
